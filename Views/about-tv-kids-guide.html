About TV Kids Guide

At TV Kids, we believe that parenting can be easier, and growing up can be fun. We are parents ourselves, and constantly looking for opportunities for enrichment and fun for our kids. As it turns out, there are lots of options in the Treasure Valley. We thought it might be easier to explore options if there were one website dedicated to showcasing all of the summer camps that serve the Treasure Valley.

We're just getting started, and we know many listings are missing from our service. If you think we've missed something, please let us know and we will be sure to add it to the list.
