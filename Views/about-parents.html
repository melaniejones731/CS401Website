Parent Frequently Asked Questions

A camp I know about isn't listed on your site.

We are constantly adding information to the site in an effort to provide a complete listing of all the camps that serve the Treasure Valley. Please send us information and we will list it!

How can I know the camps listed on this site are safe?

We recommend that parents inquire with camps and see if they are certified by the American Camp Association (ACA) and/or the Idaho Department of Health and Welfare.

How do I register for a camp?

We provide a website link for each camp. If you are interested in registering for a camp, visit the website and follow their process for registration.

There are camps listed on your website that are already full.

We are unable to monitor which camps are full or not, but hope that providing a comprehensive list of all camps is useful to parents at the start of the planning season.
